this is the front end of public forum project which is developed for fastians for public discussion on general topics and report lost item.
users from different batches could get on issues faced by the students allover the batches.
modules included are :
login
signup
user authentication(specification pattern)
generaldiscussion :
	view general discussion posts(specification design pattern with designation specification and topic specification)
		comment on posts
	make general discussion posts
		comment on your own post 
	view your own posts

lostreport:
	view lostreport posts(specification design pattern with designation specification and item specification)
		comment on posts
	make lostreport posts
		comment on your own post 
	view your own posts
